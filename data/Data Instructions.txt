1. Traveller Volumes by Port of Entry (Canada)
Source: Government of Canada (https://open.canada.ca/data/en/dataset/1b1c2b92-b388-47d9-87d4-01aee8d3c3e4/resource/22653cdd-d1e2-4c04-9d11-61b5cdd79b4e)
Description: Monthly traveller volumes by transportation mode and port of entry.
Access: Due to occasional API access issues, the dataset is pre-downloaded and stored as a CSV file in the data/ folder. The project reads it locally for reproducibility.


2. Border Crossing Entry Data (U.S.)
Source: U.S. Department of Transportation (Bureau of Transportation Statistics) (https://data.bts.gov/Research-and-Statistics/Border-Crossing-Entry-Data/keg4-3bc2/data_preview)
Description: Records the number of vehicles, equipment, passengers, or pedestrians crossing into the U.S. by port and date.
Access: Fetched directly using an API call: https://data.bts.gov/resource/keg4-3bc2.json?$limit=500000
